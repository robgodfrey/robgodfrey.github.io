Jubilee Green Consultation

The Jubilee Green Management Committee is currently undertaking a village wide consultation about Jubilee Green. Questionnaires are being delivered to every household in Wing between the 12th April and the 19th April, and will then subsequently be collected between the 19th April and the 3rd May 2009.

The main purpose of the consultation is to establish a body of evidence to support grant applications to funding bodies such as WREN, AVDC Community Chest, and the National Lottery funds. This grant money will be used to restore Jubilee Green and the childrens play area.
