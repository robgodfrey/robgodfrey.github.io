At Sixteen opens in Wing

Shop @ Sixteen

Come to visit us for our range of one- off pieces of painted furniture, vintage inspired gifts and homeware.
Christmas stock now in store. We also have a range of vintage/retro clothing, accessories and jewellery in our back room.
Opening hours are Monday to Wednesday 930 – 5, Saturday 10-5, Sunday 11 – 4. Late night shopping till 8pm Thursday and Friday. Weekday lunchtime periods please access through Window World showroom.

Ladybirds Nearly New Sale Sat 18th September

A massive thank you to everyone that supported our sale at the village hall, it was a massive success and we raised a record £600 for the toddler group! This money will now fund our Christmas Party on Saturday 4th of December for the children and we will also be buying some new toys for our weekly session, we are already planning a new musical area! The next nearly new sale will be in Spring 2011, watch this space!

Work Begins on New Play Area

Wing Parish Council are very pleased to announce that work has now begun to replace the play area on Jubilee Green. Contractors from Wicksteed Playscapes will be working on site for the next 4 to 5 weeks, and are due to complete in mid November.
