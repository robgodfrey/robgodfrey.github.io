Wing Village - Fostering http://www.wingvillage.org.uk/taxonomy/term/15/0 en Parents and Children Together - Fostering http://www.wingvillage.org.uk/node/137 <p>There is a recognised shortage of 10,000 Foster Carers in the UK. PACT (Parents And Children Together), founded in 1911, is also known as the Oxford Diocesan Council for Social Work inc. We are a registered charity working to improve life chances for children. The focus of our work is within Berkshire, Oxfordshire and Buckinghamshire and aims to build and strengthen families.</p> <p>We are an approved Adoption and Fostering Agency. We have a history of finding safe, loving families for children in care. Here are a few facts about Fostering:<br /> 1. Did you know you don’t have to be married to become a Foster Carer?<br /> 2. You don’t have to have children already although you do need to have experience of caring for children.<br /> 3. Did you know you get paid a Fostering Allowance of £335.70 a week to be a Foster Carer which is tax free and won’t affect any other benefits?<br /> 4. Most of the children who need fostering are known as ‘hard to place’ children, they will usually be 8+ years of age.</p> <p>Fostering is a hard but very rewarding job.
