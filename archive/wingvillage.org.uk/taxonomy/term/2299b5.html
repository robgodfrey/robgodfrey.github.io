BT Race To Infinity

BT Race To Infinity

Do you want Superfast Fibre Optic Broadband in Wing?

BT is running a competition to enable fibre optic broadband to 5 lucky areas. The winners can expect up to 40Mb broadband. For Wing that's about 40 times quicker than current speeds.

For a chance to Win it for Wing please vote, it takes just a minute.

Go to www.racetoinfinity.bt.com enter your postcode and vote. We have until 31st December.
