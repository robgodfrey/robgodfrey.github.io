The Cock Inn

Gary and Karen are the new managers of the Cock Inn and already feel at home here. Why not drop in and say hello!

Opening times are Monday - Saturday 12noon - 11pm and Sunday 12noon - 10.30pm

Food is served every day from 12noon - 9pm and on a Sunday we have a carvery from 12non until it runs out, restaurant shuts at 8pm but the carvery is usually gone by 6pm so it is always best to book for a Sunday dinner as we are getting very busy on a Sunday.

We still have the Diamond Club menu for the over 50's Monday - Friday, 2 courses for £6.95. We also have a lunch menu Monday - Saturday 12noon to 5pm (sandwich, chips and a drink) for £4.99. We also have our 2-£12 deal on every day, including the carvery. On Tuesdays kids eat for a £1 when dining with an adult.

2nd and 4th Thursday of every month is our Quiz night, it starts at 8.30pm and is £1 per person.

We will also be holding farmer's markets, fetes and car boot sales during the year.

Contact them on 01296 752210
