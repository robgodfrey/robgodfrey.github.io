The Village

Welcome to the website for Wing village, Bucks, where you will find information about the village of Wing, the parish council, local organisations and Wing related news and events.

Home to around 2900 residents, Wing is a lively community with lots going on.

This web site is being decommisioned

Please note this site is no longer maintained, and will be archived in the coming weeks. There are a number of alternate actively maintained web sites serving Wing village.

http://wingparish.wordpress.com/

http://www.wingparishcouncil.org/

Thanks to everyone that has supported the site over the years and I hope it provided a useful resource.
