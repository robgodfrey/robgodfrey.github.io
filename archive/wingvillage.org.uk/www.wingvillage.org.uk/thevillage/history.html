History

Wing Heritage Project

Wing Parish Council are currently trying to piece together the History of Wing for the Neighbourhood Plan. There is a website http://wingheritageproject.wordpress.com/ and our next archaeological dig is on the 11th May. We are looking for volunteers or those who are interested in the history of the area. If you are interested please email

Wing History

Alex Coles has been researching the genealogy of the people of Wing for a number of years. She manages the Wing One Place Study website and associated Winging It blog. If you are researching your family tree and have relatives who lived in Wing in days gone by then you should definitely take a look at Alex's site.

There are also some old photos of the village and villagers here.
